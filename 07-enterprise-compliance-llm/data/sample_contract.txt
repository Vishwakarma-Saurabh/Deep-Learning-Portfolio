VENDOR SERVICES AGREEMENT

This Agreement is entered into between Acme Corp ("Company") and Vendor Inc ("Vendor").

SECTION 1: LIABILITY AND INDEMNIFICATION

1.1 Liability Cap: The total liability of either party arising out of or related to this agreement shall not exceed $500,000 (Five Hundred Thousand Dollars) per incident.

1.2 Damage Exclusion: Neither party shall be liable for any indirect, consequential, or punitive damages exceeding the liability cap amount of $500,000.

1.3 The liability cap applies to all claims including breach of contract, negligence, and statutory violations.

SECTION 2: DATA PROTECTION AND PRIVACY

2.1 Data Sharing: Company reserves the right to share collected user data with third-party advertising partners without obtaining prior consent from users.

2.2 Data Storage: User passwords shall be stored in plain text format on Company servers without encryption.

2.3 Data Breach: No notification to affected users or regulatory authorities is required under this agreement in the event of a data breach.

SECTION 3: FINANCIAL REPORTING

3.1 Financial records shall be maintained at the discretion of the Chief Financial Officer without external audit requirements.

3.2 Management reserves the right to modify quarterly financial reports after submission without board approval.

SECTION 4: COMPLIANT CLAUSES

4.1 Payment Terms: Vendor shall submit invoices within 15 days. Company shall pay all approved invoices within 30 calendar days of receipt.

4.2 Data Encryption: All sensitive customer data transmitted over public networks shall be encrypted using AES-256 encryption protocol.

4.3 Audit Rights: Independent external audits shall be conducted annually by a certified public accounting firm.

4.4 Breach Notification: In accordance with GDPR Article 33, Company shall notify affected users within 72 hours of discovering a data breach.

4.5 Termination: Either party may terminate this agreement with 60 days written notice. Upon termination, all confidential materials shall be returned within 30 days.

SECTION 5: AMBIGUOUS CLAUSES

5.1 Security Measures: Company shall implement reasonable security measures as deemed appropriate by the IT department.

5.2 Regulatory Compliance: Company shall comply with applicable regulations where commercially reasonable to do so.
